┌────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ STEP 2: DEPLOY TO RAILWAY (5 minutes)                         │
└────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

1. Go to: https://railway.app
2. Click "Start a New Project"
3. Click "Deploy from GitHub repo"
4. Authorize GitHub (allow Railway to access your repos)
5. Select "google-maps-scraper" repository
6. Wait 3-5 minutes (Railway installs everything automatically)
7. Click "Settings" tab
8. Click "Generate Domain"
9. Copy your URL (e.g., your-app.up.railway.app)

   OR paste URLs directly

5. Download CSV when done!
